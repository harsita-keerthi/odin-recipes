Chocolate Milkshake

Here is a recipe for a smooth, creamy, and sweet chocolate milkshake that is perfect for summer. It will be perfect for those with a sweet tooth as well as those who want something cold and refreshing after a hot summer day.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 2 tablespoons hot fudge sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups chocolate sorbet

Steps

  1. cut vanilla ice cream and chocolate sorbet into large chunks
  2. let vanilla ice cream and chocolate sorbet cool at room temperature for 5-10 minutes
  3. if fudge sauce was refrigerated, microwave for a few seconds in a small dish
  4. blend all ingredients together in a blender and puree until smooth
  5. pour into chilled glasses and serve
