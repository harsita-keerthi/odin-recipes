Buttermilk Biscuit

Here is the recipe for a tall, flaky, and tender buttermlk biscuit. It has the perfect texture and is perfect for dinner. It will go along well with other dishes such as mashed potatoes and gravy.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all purpose flour plus more for dusting
  • 2 teaspoons granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teasppon fine salt
  • 6 tablespoons cold, unsalted butter, cut into small cubes
  • 1 cup buttermilk, chilled

Steps

  1. in a large bowl, combine all dry ingredients
  2. using fingertips, combine bitter into dry mixture until it is a coarse meal
  3. stir in buttermilk
  4. turn dough onto floured surface and dust the dough as well
  5. pat down dough into 1/2 inch thick rectangle
  6. fold dough into thirds, rotating 90 degrees between each fold
  7. pat the dough into a final 1 inch thick rectangle
  8. use something circular to cut out the biscuits
  9. freeze uncooked dough while oven preheats to 400 degrees
  10. bake the biscuits for 12-15 minutes until golden brown
  11. serve while hot
